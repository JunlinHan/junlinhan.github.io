Research Interests

My research focuses on multimodal foundation models, including multimodal large language models and generative models, as well as the interactions between them.

I am particularly interested in a vision-first path toward general intelligence, where visual pre-training plays a central role.

Multimodal foundation models
Selected projects: Visual priors in LLM pre-training, Llama 4
Generative models, especially 3D and video
Selected projects: Scaling up 3D generation, High-quality 3D asset generation, Scaling neural rendering
Integration and interaction between modalities
Selected projects: Unified pre-training, LLMs for 3D world modeling
